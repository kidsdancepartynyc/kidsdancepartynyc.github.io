FAQ

Do kids need dance experience?

No. Every party is beginner-friendly and designed so every child can participate and feel confident.

How much space do we need?

Enough open space for kids to move safely. We confirm the space during a quick 15-minute FaceTime or Zoom consultation.

Can you come to our home or venue?

Yes. We come to homes, apartment buildings, schools, community centers, parks, and event venues. Studio hosting is also available for an additional $200.

The standard party accommodates up to 25 children.
